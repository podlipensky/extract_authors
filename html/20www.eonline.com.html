    Kelly Rowland Debuts New Song Featuring Beyoncé; Plus, Singer Breaks Down Crying Onstage—Watch Now

    by Rebecca Macatee Today 8:25 AM PDT

    Loading...

    Kelly Rowland's got a case of the feels, y'all!

    On Sunday, the Destiny's Child singer was overcome with emotion during a performance of her new single, "Dirty Laundry," onstage in Washington, D.C. Beyoncé's BFF actually had to stop singing, burying her face in her hands as she broke down crying. Fortunately, the audience began chanting Kelly's name, encouraging her to continue on with the song.

    VIDEO: Kelly gushes over Bey's baby girl Blue Ivy

    And even though "Dirty Laundry" hints at some jealousy between Michelle and Bey, these two women—plus Michelle Williams—clearly still adore one another. On Monday, Kelly dropped by Blis FM to premiere "You Changed," a track from her upcoming album that features her Destiny's Child bandmates.

    Kelly's new album, Talk a Good Game, comes out June 18. Who wants to bet Beyoncé's already singing along to her advanced copy?!
